Alchemical Healing

Apply to work together

This application is for people who are ready to do deep work — the kind that moves through the body, the story, the lineage, and the places that haven't shifted no matter what else you've tried.

I work with a small number of people at this level and I'm selective, because this kind of work requires the right conditions on both sides.

I read every application personally and I will be in touch. There's no wrong way to fill this out — just be honest. Honesty is how we find each other.

How It Works

What happens after you apply

01
You fill out the application
Answer as honestly as you can. There are no right answers — only true ones. The act of filling it out is itself part of the process.
02
I read it personally
Every application comes to me directly. I sit with what you've shared before responding.
03
We have a conversation
If it feels like a good fit, I'll reach out to schedule a short call — so we can both sense whether this is the right time and the right match.
04
We begin
If we both feel yes, we'll schedule your first session. The work starts before you arrive — in the intention you bring to this application.
